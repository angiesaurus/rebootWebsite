Technology + Entrepreneurs + Researchers = Safer Communities

Our Mission

To support and contribute to safe, prosperous communities.

We do this by matching our technology fellows with partnering advocacy organizations and policy think-tanks to build public safety solutions with communities in need.

Our Pillars

Reboot Weekends

Our Reboot events consist of hackathons and workshops focused on building public safety solutions and prototypes with local changemakers and advocacy partners.

R & D

Reboot Safety invests in the highest quality research to shape our public safety proposals, solutions, and development. We partner with think-tanks and research groups looking to create solutions based on their findings.

Public Education

We believe that knowledge is power and we aim to share everything that we know and learn through workshops, writings, panels, videos, online material, and more.

Community Partner

Reboot Safety acts as a hub for people working towards a equitable societies. Through partnership with local advocacy organizations, universities, companies, government, and individuals, Reboot Safety brings together experts from intersecting disciplines for collaboration and community support.
